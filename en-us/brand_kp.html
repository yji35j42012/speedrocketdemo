Brand Profile

Fragrance is part of the first impression you leave, often crucial to any interaction. We realized that renowned perfumes are often very expensive, and therefore hard for first time users to start. We therefore decided to create a perfume with outstanding quality but a entry level price tag. KLOWER PANDOR was born with the concept of Pandora’s Flower Knowledge, and we hope like the box, each will open a whole new scenario for you to explore. What we are creating at KP is a fragrance for you to keep, and create a memory for you and the people you encounter to cherish.
